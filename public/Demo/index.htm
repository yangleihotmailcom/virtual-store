Summary

This is a web application named "Lei's Virtual Store", in virtual store, there are lots of products you can search/view and add them into cart.
Actually, all data in back-end comes from amazon and increased/updated automatically during your surfing.
It uses the Amazon Product Advertising API to communicate with Amazon.
These videos are in Youtube, please click play button to view them.

Youtube videos have varying degrees of quality, The default value depends on your internet connection, change it to 720p to get high quality video after clicking the play button.

Home

  • Each page contains header, footer, left navigator and page content.
  • The header contains logo icon, you can go to home page any time by clicking it.
  • The header contains "shopping Cart", you can view shopping cart content any time when you mouse over it.
  • The banner shows random products and slide left automatically.
  • In left navigator, it contains search form and categories links, up to 46 categories for the moment and will be increased automatically when new category/product added.
  • In page content, 46 categories are grouped in several sections, 10 items are showed for each category.
  • All images are clickable and a quick view dialog occurs which contains simple information about the product.
  • You can add it to cart, go to details page or amazon product page in the quick view dialog.

Search Page

  • In left side, in search form, select a category and enter keywords, you can get maximum 100 items for the keywords.
  • In result page, you can find how many items returned and they are paginated.
  • The search is slow since Amazon Product Advertising API used here ( I will cache the result to speed it up later).
  • Each search result page contains 10 items, You can click any item to quickly view or add it to cart directly.
  • This video shows you the result when you search keywords "note" and "computer".

Log In/Out

  • In the header, click "Log in", then you can register and login in popup dialog.
  • This video show you the form validation for registering new user and shopping cart management for the specific user.
  • If you are new user, you need to create new accout, the captcha is used in case auto-register from hacker.

Shopping Cart

  • In the header, mouse over "shopping cart" and click button "shopping cart" in popup window, you go to shopping cart page.
  • You can update quantity or remove any item, or even empty whole cart.
  • Before removing/emptying, you need to confirm your operation.
  • Ajax(jQuery) is used here, no page transfers and the date is updated in back-end.
  • Almost all images are clickable and the item can be added into cart in popup dialog.
  • The cart is persistent, other user can not see your cart.

Category Landing and Product Details

  • whatever you do, once you find an item and click it, in popup dialog, click button "More Details", you will go to product details page.
  • Product details page includes more information for the product.
  • Sometimes, product details page is slow if it contains accessories/similar items since extra web service call is made for them by Ajax.
  • In left side, click any category, you will go to category page.
  • The category page shows the items for and only for this category.

Amazon Checkout

  • Add some items into your cart, go to shopping cart page.
  • Click button "Checkout", the login form occurs if you are not login.
  • After login, you may find the more items in your shopping cart, these items are in your private cart you added before.
  • If everything goes well, new window is opened and you go to amazon shopping cart successfully.
  • Sometimes, the item was expired/insufficient, an error message maybe pop-up, you can click "continue" button to ignore it, and item does not exist in amazon shopping cart.
  • Click button "continue" and go ahead in amazon shopping cart page.
